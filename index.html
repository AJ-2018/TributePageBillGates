Bill Gates

Let's get to know more about Bill Gates:

  • 1955 - Born in Seattle, Washington on October 28.
  • 1968 - At the age of 13, Gates enrolled in the Lakeside School.
  • 1973 - Gates enrolled at Harvard College as a pre-law major.
  • 1975 - By working day and night, Bill Gates and Paul Allen created the first version of Microsoft BASIC for the Altair 8800.
  • 1976 - The trade name "Microsoft" was registered.
  • 1981 - Microsoft was re-incorporated in Washington State and Bill Gates became the President and Board Chairman of Microsoft.
  • 1989 - Bill Gates founded Corbis, a digital imaging company.
  • 1995 - Bill Gates along with Nathan Myhrvold and Peter Rinearson wrote his first book 'The Road Ahead', detailing how new technologies will govern the future.
  • 1999 - Bill Gates has also written a book named 'Business @ the Speed of Thought', which discusses how business and technology are integrated and shows how digital infrastructures and information networks can help getting an edge of the competition.
  • 2000 - Bill and Melinda Gates founded Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation (BMGF), which was identified by the Funds for NGOs company in 2013, as the world's wealthiest charitable foundation.
  • 2005 - Bill Gates was made an Honorary Knight Commander of the Order of the British Empire (KBE) by Queen Elizabeth II for his contributions to United Kingdom.
  • 2007 - Gates received honorary doctorate from Harvard University in June.
